Fleet Partners

We're thrilled that you're considering becoming a part of our dynamic team at GFL.
Whether you're looking to join us as a rider, fleet owner, or as a business partner, we have exciting opportunities waiting for you. Join us and be a vital part of our mission to deliver excellence, speed, and reliability to our valued customers.

Why Choose GFL?

  • 1.
    Rider Opportunities

    Are you an enthusiastic individual with a passion for the open road? Join our team of riders and become the face of our company. We offer competitive compensation, flexible schedules, and the chance to be a part of a tight-knit community. Your journey with us begins here.

  • 2.
    Fleet Owner Partnerships

    Own a fleet of vehicles and looking to expand your horizons? Partner with us to leverage our extensive network and expertise brought together by our mobile application. Benefit from consistent business opportunities, streamlined operations, and access to cutting-edge technology. Together, we can drive success.

  • 3.
    Business Collaborations

    Are you a business owner looking to enhance your logistics capabilities? Partner with GFL to unlock a world of efficiency and reliability. We offer tailored solutions, state-of-the-art tracking systems, and a dedicated support team. Elevate your logistics game with us.

Why Join Our Team?

  • Innovation

    We're at the forefront of logistics technology, continuously evolving to provide the best solutions for our clients and partners.

  • Growth

    GFL is on an upward trajectory, and we want you to grow with us. Your success is our success.

  • Community

    Join a diverse and supportive community of professionals who are passionate about what they do.

  • Training & Development

    We invest in our team members, offering training and development opportunities to help you excel in your role.
